St. Edward Parish BSA

The St. Edward Parish BSA program is a Boy Scouts of America program chartered by St. Edward Roman Catholic Parish in Jeffersontown, KY. We are members of the Cherokee District in the Lincoln Heritage Council.

While we are working on providing better content here, please visit the following sites for more information on our programs:

  • Troop 175 - Our Scouting BSA, scout-led program for children ages 11-18.
  • Pack 175 - Our Cub Scouts program for children in grades K-5.

For more information on our programs or how to join, please contact our scout leaders via the Facebook pages linked above.
